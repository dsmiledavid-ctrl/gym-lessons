# LESSON 01 — Forward Roll & Soccer Basics

**Tumbling:** Forward Roll  |  **Sport:** Soccer  |  **Muscle:** Core  |  **Relay:** Shuttle Run
**Equipment:** 4 large mats · balls (1/child) · 6 cones · 6 hoops · beanbags · music

---

## REFERENCE CARD

**ARRIVAL — 5 min**
Scatter beanbags, tennis balls, 2–3 hoops. Free play. Observe the group.
*(Set up two mat lines along the wall while kids play.)*

**WARM-UP A — 5 min** | Aerobics
March · High knees · Jumping jacks · Grapevine R/L · Side step touch — 30 sec each

**WARM-UP B — 3 min** | Step-Touch Bounce (hip hop)
1. Feet only — step R, together, step L, together
2. Add bounce on the landing
3. Add loose shoulder drop
4. Music on — "Find YOUR version"

**TUMBLING — 8 min** | Forward Roll
Cue: *"Look at your belly button!"*
1. Shape only — squat · hands flat · chin to chest · hold
2. First roll — push off feet, roll through, walk back
3. Clean stand — pop up without hands, arms up (gymnast finish)
4. Two-in-a-row (advanced)

**SPORT — 10 min** | Soccer Basics
Setup: 2 cone goals at opposite ends, 1 ball per child
1. Dribbling (3 min) — inside foot, little taps, slow jog
2. Wall passing (3 min) — kick + trap, plant foot next to ball
3. Shooting at goal (4 min) — 3 dribble taps, then shoot low

**MUSCLE — 5 min** | Core Circuit — 20–25 sec each
1. Plank hold — body straight, tummy tight
2. Dead bug — alternate arm/leg, back flat on floor, slow
3. Bicycle crunches — elbow to opposite knee, smooth
4. Superman — arms + legs up from face-down, hold 10 sec
5. V-sit — lean back, feet up, hold V shape 10 sec

**RELAY — 10 min** | Shuttle Run
Setup: 2 teams, 3 hoops per line spaced 1.5m apart, matching beanbags
1. Colour match — learning pace, 1 beanbag at a time
2. Speed round — same rules, fastest team wins
3. Reverse — bring all beanbags back to start
4. Memory — swap colour assignment ("red goes in the blue hoop")
5. BONUS: do a forward roll before dropping beanbag in hoop

**COOL-DOWN — 4 min**
Breathe × 3 · Forward fold · Butterfly stretch · Lying stretch (10 sec silence)
Send-off: *"Look at your belly button — try it at home. See you next week."*

---

## EXERCISE GUIDE

**STEP-TOUCH BOUNCE**
Step R foot out, bring L to meet it with a small bounce. Step L, bring R with bounce. Add a loose shoulder drop on each landing. Teach in 4 stages: feet only (no music) → add bounce → add shoulder → music on. Once music starts, stop counting — let them feel the beat. Shtick: fake getting confused mid-song and let the kids correct you.

**FORWARD ROLL**
5 points: squat · hands flat shoulder-width, fingers forward · chin to chest ("look at your belly button") · push off feet, roll on back of head with rounded spine · stand, arms up.
Corrections: rolling sideways → chin not tucked | landing on top of head → slow down and re-demo.
Support: one hand on upper back for direction, one under knees for tuck. Never force.
5–6 yr: allow hands to push up to standing. Nervous: rock-and-roll from sitting first. Advanced: two-in-a-row, standing start, run-up.

**SOCCER**
Dribbling: show the toe kick first, make it look bad, then demo inside foot. "Tap, don't boot." Keep ball close, small taps.
Wall passing: main cue is "plant foot NEXT to the ball." Kick through the middle. Trap with sole of foot.
Shooting: teacher stands in goal. Big dramatic saves for some, let others score easily. Count hat tricks out loud. "Aim low."

**CORE CIRCUIT**
Plank: hands under shoulders, body in one line, tummy pulled in. "Don't tent your bum up, don't sag in the middle."
Dead bug: back must stay flat on floor at all times. Lower opposite arm + leg slowly. "Slow is harder than fast."
Bicycle: hands behind head, don't pull the neck. Elbow to opposite knee, smooth pedalling rhythm.
Superman: lie face down, arms forward. Lift arms AND legs off the floor at the same time. Hold 10 sec.
V-sit: sit, lean slightly back, lift both feet. Hold the V shape. Arms out to the sides for balance.

**SHUTTLE RUN**
Demo slowly with one volunteer before starting. Get team names before Round 1. Between rounds: give one team a dramatic over-the-top pep talk — they lose anyway, act genuinely baffled.
Round 4 (memory): announce the colour swap just before GO for maximum chaos.
Round 5 (bonus): roll at the first hoop before dropping the beanbag — connects back to tumbling, kids love the callback.

**COOL-DOWN**
Wait for full quiet before starting. Breathe: in through nose, hold, long out through mouth. Forward fold: legs straight, reach forward, no bouncing. Butterfly: feet together, knees out, gentle press. Lying stretch: arms overhead, stretch long — then 10 seconds of intentional silence.
